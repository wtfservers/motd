Welcome to TTT - 24/7 - Vanilla - Minecraft - Australia!

Trouble in Terrorist Town (TTT) is a gamemode included with Garry's Mod. The game is about a group of "terrorists" who have traitors among them, out to kill everyone who's not a traitor. A small number of players are selected as Traitors, who have to kill all the Innocent players (ie. the rest of the players).

Those innocents know they are in the majority, but they do not know who is Traitor and who is not. The Traitors must use the element of surprise and their special equipment, if they are to succeed. The Innocent just have to survive, which means finding out who the Traitors are and killing them before they kill you.

1. Server Rules

  • Do not abuse commands e.g. !unstuck or find/use exploits to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not harass or bully other players.
    • Doxing (revealing a players real-life identity) a player will result in a permanent ban from the Community.
  • Do not intentionally annoy people for your own amusement.
  • Do not metagame. This means using any form of communication to reveal otherwise unknown information about Traitors and player locations to those who are still alive.
  • Do not RDM (Random Death Match). This means killing other players without a valid reason. Leaving after unpunished RDM will result in a ban.
  • Do not spam your microphone (this includes continuous noises, talking in a loud manner, playing music through your microphone).
  • Do not take it upon yourself to enforce rules (e.g. killing rule breakers). Server staff will deal with rule breakers.
  • Do not use cheats, hax, scripts or any other type of game modification. Doing so will result in a permanent ban from the Community.
  • False reports or violent language in a report are neither helpful nor tolerated, and will result in a slay. Truthfully and calmly explaining a situation is more helpful to staff when handling reports.
  • Have a pronounceable and typable name. Failing to change your name upon the request of staff will result in a kick.
  • Players must not attempt to impersonate TTT staff and/or players in anyway (unless you have permission).
  • Points spent in the Points Shop can not be reimbursed by staff.
  • Prolonged periods of inactivity will result in being kicked.
  • Threatening (or undertaking) to DDOS the server or any of our players will result in a permanent ban from the Community.
  • When using any donator commands (!gag, !mute) you must inform staff via admin chat (@) before doing so.
  • You are held accountable for the actions made by your own account, and you will be punished as such.

2. Gameplay Rules

  • Destroying a health station or moving it to where it is inaccessible is a KOSable offence.
  • Destroying or rendering a Traitor tester inoperative is a KOSable offence, unless done pre-round.
  • Killing or injuring players with map specific weapons or traps is KOSable.
  • Killing or injuring with props is only allowed for living Traitors.
  • Traitor baiting (e.g. discharging a weapon near a player without harmful intent) is not KOSable, however being killed by the victim will not be considered RDM. Deliberate traitor baiting will result in consequences.

Innocent/Detective

  • Do not shoot or kill somebody without solid evidence, such as a KOS, DNA from a body, or witnessing an event first-hand. Falsely KOSing a player will result in players calling a KOS on you.
  • Invalid grounds to kill or shoot someone as an Innocent/Detective:
    • Suspicious activity.
    • The player is AFK or not moving.
    • Not responding to a life check, unless in overtime.
    • Being near an unidentified body or refusing to identify it.
    • Being hit in crossfire.
    • Shooting someone unannounced.
  • Throwing grenades that have the ability to harm players, without proof of traitorous actions, is not permitted. Warning must be given before throwing a grenade.
  • Upon obtaining a T weapon, announce it in chat.
  • When disarming C4, give sufficient warning as to your location and when you will do so.

Traitor

  • Do not delay. In overtime, stalling or camping will result in a slay.
  • Warn fellow Traitors about any harmful actions (e.g. T traps) and give sufficient time for them to vacate the targeted area.
